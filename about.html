The Legend of Nano

Nano is a Seelyham Terrier. He is short, stubborn, and almost certainly the most important dog of his generation. The world has long agreed that Nano is the greatest dog ever. We simply built the trading card game to confirm what was already true.

The Universe

Nano's Ascension chronicles every moment that matters — the naps, the zoomies, the surprise attacks from leaf piles, the late-night programming sessions that nobody asked for. Each card is a chapter, and every expansion is a new act in an ongoing legend.

How To Play

Each player builds a deck of Characters, Actions, and Treats. Treats fuel your plays. Characters do the work. Actions bend the rules in your favor. The first player to outwit and outlast the other claims the day — until tomorrow, when the cycle resets and Nano demands breakfast again.

Rarities and Variants

Cards range from Common to Rare, with the occasional Mythic hiding in deeper expansions. Variants — Foil, Holo, and Etched — are not just prettier; they hit harder. A Holo Rare carries a 1.25× multiplier on its base stats. The Etched ones are vanishingly rare.

What's Next

New expansions arrive when Nano decides they should. Until then: open packs, build decks, find your favorite, and let the greatest dog ever rest a little easier knowing his story is in capable hands.
